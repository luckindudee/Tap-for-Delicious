Chicken Burger with Potato Chips | LKR1100

Mix ground chicken, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, onion, egg, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Moisten hands and shape chicken mixture, 2 tablespoons at a time, into flat, oval-shaped patties. Spread remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs into a shallow dish. Roll patties in bread crumbs to coat. Also, with thin slice of potato that has been either deep fried and baked until crunchy.
